My Experiences

Embracing diverse learning opportunities has shaped my journey, blending technical mastery with a profound appreciation for cultural knowledge.


Agnihotra Yagna

Every evening at sunset, I perform the Aghnihotra Yagna. This ancient Vedic ritual involves offering oblations into a specially prepared fire, which is believed to purify the environment and promote healing. The practice of Aghnihotra Yagna is an important part of my daily routine and spiritual discipline.

Sharing Knowledge About Indian Knowledge System

During a session with students at a school, I addressed the discrepancy in the Indian knowledge system's emphasis on Western cultural history over Indian history. This discussion aimed to promote a more balanced educational approach,advocating for the inclusion of India's rich historical heritage alongside global perspectives.

BOOTCAMP TEACHING

I had the privilege of teaching the fundamentals of the C programming language to new students at a specialized bootcamp organized by Royal Technosoft. Guiding eager learners through concepts like variables, loops, and functions was a rewarding experience, fostering their understanding of foundational programming principles and preparing them for future challenges in software development.
